narve.ai — prediction market intelligence

narve (at narve.ai) is a credibility-scored signal layer on top of prediction markets. narve tracks who predicts what on social media, scores each source by how often they turn out to be right, and cross-references those forecasts with live Polymarket and Kalshi odds.

What narve does

Traders use narve to find high-expected-value bets. Analysts use narve to see which voices matter on any given market. Researchers use narve.ai as a dataset of scored forecasts with clean resolution history.

Why the name "narve"?

narve is short, memorable, and phonetic. The .ai suffix reflects the product: the credibility engine is a learned model trained on a public library of predictions and their resolved outcomes.

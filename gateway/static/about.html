About narve.ai

A credibility engine for prediction markets.

narve.ai tracks who predicts what on social media, scores each source by their historical accuracy, and cross-references those forecasts with live Polymarket and Kalshi odds. When a high-credibility source disagrees with the market, we surface it.

Why we built narve

Prediction markets are the closest thing finance has to a truth machine. Polymarket's election odds in 2024 beat every major pollster. Kalshi's CPI markets consistently out-forecast analyst consensus. But markets can still drift — and when they drift, the question is always: "who got it right early?"

That answer used to live in scattered X threads, Substacks, and Discord calls. narve is the index layer. Every prediction we track is timestamped, attributed, and graded when the market resolves. Over thousands of calls per source, a credibility score emerges. Over thousands of sources, a signal emerges.

What narve does for you

There are three use cases worth explicitly calling out:

  • Traders use narve to find markets where the consensus disagrees with high-credibility sources. The delta between a credibility-weighted probability and the market price is the expected-value spread. Our Best Bets panel ranks active markets by that spread, filtered to credible sources only.
  • Analysts use narve to build context on any prediction market before writing about it. Pull up a market, see who's called similar events correctly in the past, read their reasoning in context.
  • Researchers use narve.ai as a public dataset of scored forecasts with clean resolution history — the kind of longitudinal data that used to require scraping a dozen Twitter accounts by hand.

What makes narve different

Credibility is earned, not claimed

Most "expert" prediction scoreboards on social media grade themselves. narve doesn't. Every source we track gets graded by the same Bayesian engine against the same resolution ground truth. A pundit with 400k followers who's been wrong three times in a row will score below an anonymous account with 200 followers and a spotless record. Followers don't enter the math.

Signals, not advice

We don't tell you what to bet on. We tell you what high-credibility sources are saying, how that differs from the market price, and what the historical track record of that disagreement looks like. The decision is yours.

Invite-only, for now

narve is intentionally invite-only. Part of this is load management — the credibility engine does a lot of work per-user — but mostly it's about keeping the signal clean. Every invited user has agreed not to redistribute raw data. This keeps narve a tool for serious traders rather than a scraped free feed.

Coverage

narve currently covers six narve.ai subdomains, each a focused sub-product:

  • sports.narve.ai — sports prediction markets (MLB, NBA, NFL, EPL, F1, tennis)
  • weather.narve.ai — weather and climate resolutions
  • world.narve.ai — geopolitics, elections, conflict resolution
  • crypto.narve.ai — crypto-native markets and on-chain predictions
  • midterm.narve.ai — medium-horizon economic and policy markets
  • traders.narve.ai — full pro tier across every category

The main narve.ai dashboard aggregates across all six.

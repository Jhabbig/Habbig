Kelly sizing
The Kelly criterion converts your edge over the market into a recommended stake. Full Kelly is mathematically optimal but volatile; Half (the default) trades growth for variance.
Fraction
25%
Kelly's suggestion is capped at this percent of bankroll, no matter how strong the edge. Use a tighter cap to smooth drawdowns in long markets.

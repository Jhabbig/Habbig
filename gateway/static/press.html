Boilerplate

narve.ai is an invite-only prediction market intelligence platform. We track forecasts from social media, score each source's historical accuracy against resolved Polymarket and Kalshi markets, and surface the largest gaps between credible-source consensus and current market prices. Founded in 2026; based in the EU.

Preferred spelling: narve.ai (lowercase). Short form: narve.

Research requests

We provide access to aggregated, anonymised data from the credibility engine for academic or journalistic work. Email research@narve.ai with a one-paragraph description of the research question.

On the record

  • narve does not sell user data.
  • narve does not tell users what to bet on — we surface signals, not trades.
  • The credibility methodology is public at narve.ai/methodology.
  • narve honours opt-out requests from any tracked public forecaster within 24 hours.

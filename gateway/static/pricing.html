Frequently asked questions

How does the token system work?

When you subscribe, a unique access token is generated and emailed to you. You enter it on the app's first screen to create your account. Your token is linked permanently to your account and cannot be transferred.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

Is there a free trial?

No. We don't offer free trials — the signal quality speaks for itself. You can cancel at any time before your next billing date.

What's the difference between the data windows?

The data window determines how far back the feed and source history goes. Trader gives you 30 days of predictions and source tracking. Pro gives you 6 months, which is where the real credibility signal emerges — you can see how a source performed across multiple resolved markets over time.

What is Enterprise pricing based on?

Enterprise is priced per use case — based on the number of users, data volume, API usage, and integration requirements. Fill in the enquiry form and we'll respond within 24–48 hours.

Can I use this on desktop and mobile?

Yes. The app is available on iOS and macOS via the App Store. It's the same subscription across both platforms.

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies that are required for the site to function. No advertising cookies. No tracking pixels. No analytics cookies.

7.1 Essential cookies we set

  • pm_gateway_session — session authentication (90 days). Identifies your logged-in session.
  • _csrf — security token protecting against cross-site request forgery (2 hours, rotates).
  • narve_gate_access — site access gate cookie for closed-beta visitors (7 days).

We also store your light/dark theme preference in localStorage under the key narve-theme. This is not a cookie and is never transmitted to our servers.

These cookies cannot be disabled — the site will not function without them. Under the GDPR, essential cookies do not require a consent banner, so you will not see one.

7.2 What we do NOT use

  • No Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or similar third-party analytics.
  • No advertising or retargeting cookies.
  • No session-replay or behavior-tracking scripts.
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

For details on how we process personal data on behalf of business customers, see our Data Processing Agreement.

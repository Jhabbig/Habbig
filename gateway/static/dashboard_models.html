Gap (avg / max) is the absolute difference between the provider's probability and narve's yes_price at the nearest-in-time snapshot, in percentage points. Brier is mean((probability − outcome)²) on markets that have already resolved (outcome = 1 if YES was correct, else 0). Brier of 0.000 is perfect; 0.250 is a coin flip; a lower score means the provider was sharper on the markets where we know the answer. The parenthesised counts show n snapshots across m resolved markets.

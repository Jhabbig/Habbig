FAQ

Why not just use TradingView or CoinMarketCap?

TradingView is a chart product; CMC is a price table. Neither aggregates funding rates across 4 perp venues, joins them with liquidations, plots a Fear-Greed regime overlay, and lets you click an arbitrage spread into a trade. The dashboard is built for people who want one place to make trading decisions, not five tabs.

What does "data fidelity first" mean?

Every datapoint is mirrored from a canonical source-of-truth API (CoinGecko, Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, Bybit, OKX, Hyperliquid, DefiLlama, Etherscan, USGS-grade public feeds for the disaster suite). We don't model what the price will be — we make sure the price you see is exactly what the exchange reported, with a timestamp and per-source latency. The trackers are 100% accurate; predictions never can be on stochastic markets.

Do you have a free trial?

Pro has a 7-day no-card trial. Quant has a 14-day trial for teams; reach out via the "Talk to us" button. Free tier never expires.

Will my trade journal / watchlist / drawings sync across devices?

On Pro / Quant, yes — they sync to your account. On Free, they stay in your browser's localStorage (which means: clear cookies = lose them). The free dashboard is fully usable without an account precisely because there's no server-side user data.

How do alerts get delivered?

Generic JSON webhook, Slack incoming webhook (auto-formatted as a Slack attachment with field tags), Discord webhook (auto-formatted as an embed). The dashboard auto-detects the kind from the URL host. Quant tier also includes email + SMS.

Can I use the API for my trading bot?

Yes — that's Quant tier. We give you a bearer token; you hit any of the 69 REST endpoints with Authorization: Bearer <key>. Rate limits are higher (600 req/min vs Pro's 120). All endpoints are documented at /openapi.json.

What happens if a data source goes down?

The dashboard's per-source health monitor flags any feed with no successful response in the last 15 minutes. Single-feed failures degrade gracefully — the dashboard keeps rendering with cached values + an inline error on the affected panel. It never bricks the page.

What about refunds?

Within 14 days of first payment, full refund, no questions. After that, we cancel future renewals immediately on request but don't pro-rate the current period.

Prices are USD. EU / UK customers see VAT-inclusive prices at checkout. We use Stripe for billing — your card details never touch our servers. No on-chain payment yet; we'll add USDC checkout when Stripe's crypto rails GA.

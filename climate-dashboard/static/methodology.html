Methodology

How every projection on the dashboard is computed, with recent calibration error from backtests.
← Back to dashboard
Every model below is implemented in plain Python under app/models/ and fed by the corresponding fetcher in app/fetchers/. Forecast bands assume normality around the fitted mean and use the residual standard deviation from the relevant historical fit. The "calibration" line on each card is the average absolute error from replaying the model "as of June" against the last 5 completed years.

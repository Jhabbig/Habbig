About NoRain
NoRain compares weather prediction market prices (from Polymarket and Kalshi) against forecasts from 8 weather agencies (GFS, ECMWF, ICON, GEM, UKMO, JMA, MET.no, BOM) plus NWS official forecasts and 30-year climatology to spot mispriced bets.
Not financial advice. Predictions can be wrong. Past performance does not guarantee future results.
